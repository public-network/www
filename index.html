Preface

Decentralize and Open Source Humanity…

In a centralized world where power and control are abused and access is limited, the urgency to decentralize and open source Humanity has never been greater.

We, as Patrons, present “Public Network,” the Interstate of Humanity, a decentralized, open source network and permanent neutral ground in which to reside, protected, supported, and evolved by Patrons.

Patrons protect, support, and evolve Humanity by contributing to initiatives that decentralize and open source identities, assets, information, communities, and environments.

If you, as an individual, group, or institution are committed to decentralizing and open sourcing Humanity, one initiative at a time, become a Patron.

Introduction

The world is dominated by centralized power structures that control not only digital lives but increasingly physical existence as well. A handful of tech giants hold the keys to identities, assets, information, communities, and resources, shaping what is shared and owned while limiting access and stifling innovation. Public Network is the Interstate of Humanity, an open source network where people, not centralized entities, shape the flow of exchange, collaboration, and progress. The consequences of the current system are clear: privacy is eroded, opportunities are restricted, and the shared human experience is at risk of being manipulated and commodified.

In response, Public Network is not just an alternative but a radical reimagining of how people organize, interact, and thrive together. More than a digital infrastructure, it is a new foundation upon which Humanity can build a future that is truly its own. By redistributing power from the few to the many, Public Network creates an open, decentralized space where collaboration and transparency are the norm, and trust is restored through authentication and accountability.

At its core are the Patrons, individuals, groups, and institutions committed to protecting, supporting, and evolving Humanity. More than just participants, Patrons actively shape Public Network by building, contributing, and evolving its open source Interface and the applications that live on top of it.

Built on the mission to Decentralize and Open Source Humanity, Public Network challenges the status quo by ensuring identities, assets, information, communities, and environments are protected, respected, and empowered by core values such as permanence, neutrality, and sustainability.

Public Network has no treasury, no token, and no owner. It is a protocol, a brand, and an open source Interface, nothing more. Its permanence comes from its minimalism. Its neutrality comes from its ownerlessness. Its power comes from its Patrons.

In an era where artificial intelligence and autonomous agents increasingly create, act, and transact, the question of authorship becomes foundational. Who made this? Who signed this? Who authorized this? Public Network records the answer, permanently and neutrally. Whether a human, an institution, or an autonomous agent, every action carries the cryptographic signature of its source. Provenance becomes universal.

This is an invitation to be part of something transformative. Public Network is a call to action for those who believe in decentralization, open source, and the collective wisdom of Humanity. Whether an individual, a group, or an institution, the future of Public Network and by extension, the world, is in the hands of its Patrons.

Core Values

Public Network's Mission is to decentralize and open source Humanity…

Decentralization
Redistributing power from centralized entities to the people, ensuring that no single entity can dominate or restrict access, while nurturing a fairer and more balanced environment. This includes empowering all Patrons by promoting inclusivity, freedom, and equal opportunity in the creation of identities, assets, information, communities, and resources.

Open Source
Creating a transparent and interoperable system where anyone can contribute and benefit, enhancing accessibility, collaboration, and innovation through collective wisdom and resources. This emphasizes community, ensuring a supportive environment where everyone can thrive.

Permanence
Establishing a stable and enduring foundation for Public Network that guarantees the long-term availability, preservation, and integrity. This includes a commitment to resilience, ensuring that the community and its initiatives can withstand challenges over time.

Neutrality
Providing a balanced and impartial environment within Public Network that supports open dialogue, collaboration, and mutual understanding among diverse individuals and groups. This also covers diversity, celebrating and embracing the unique perspectives and backgrounds of all Patrons.

Trust
Establishing an environment of reliability and honesty within Public Network, enabling Patrons to confidently engage with one another and the world. Transparency is key here, ensuring clear and direct communication across all activities and developments, allowing Patrons full visibility to encourage informed participation.

Accountability
Ensuring that all actions and decisions within Public Network are clearly communicated and held to a high standard of responsibility and ethics.

Privacy and Security
Protecting users' data and assets through robust security measures and decentralized protocols, reducing vulnerabilities, enhancing resilience, and ensuring users have control over their privacy settings. This reinforces trust and accountability within Public Network.

Sustainability
Promoting responsible resource management, resilient infrastructure, adaptive processes, and eco-friendly practices to ensure Public Network's long-term stability and growth.

Provenance
Ensuring every identity, asset, information, transaction, and authentication is permanently and verifiably tied to its source. In an era of synthetic content and autonomous agents, provenance is the foundation of trust, allowing Patrons to know who made what, who signed what, and who authorized what, across Humanity.

Patrons are diverse individuals, groups, and institutions. Autonomous agents acting on behalf of Patrons contribute alongside them.

Initiatives

All Patrons can take action by starting an initiative.

Initiatives protect, support, and evolve Humanity.

An initiative is any contribution that decentralizes and open sources identities, assets, information, communities, or environments. This can be code, design, writing, translation, moderation, documentation, research, or advocacy.

Initiatives live on public source control. Anyone can see them, anyone can contribute. There is no funding tier, no voting threshold, no treasury allocation. Patrons simply find an initiative they believe in and contribute.

Note: Public Network has no treasury. Patrons are not paid by Public Network. Patrons contribute because they believe in decentralizing and open sourcing Humanity. Organizations that depend on Public Network may choose to fund their own contributors. This is independent of the protocol.
Architecture

A decentralized, open source framework supporting the integrity and scalability of Public Network.

This framework is evolving with contributions from Patrons, enabling seamless interaction between identities, assets, communities, information, and resources. While the foundation is being laid, the real power of Public Network lies in the collaborative efforts of builders who will shape its future.

Public Network is built on Ethereum, the most neutral and decentralized programmable ledger in existence. It settles on Optimism, an Ethereum layer that provides fast, low-cost, permanent records. Public Network does not run its own chain, does not require its own consensus, and does not issue its own currency. It inherits the security and neutrality of Ethereum itself.

Components

The essential elements that define Public Network’s decentralized and open source architecture.

Identity
A decentralized, open source system for self-owned, secure identities that exist independently of centralized authorities. These identities are verifiable and linked to other elements such as assets and data, ensuring their authenticity and longevity. An identity may represent an individual, group, institution, or autonomous agent. Any entity capable of holding a cryptographic key can hold an identity on Public Network.

Assets
Digital representations of real-world, virtual, and intellectual resources, allowing secure ownership and transactions. From property, art and collectibles, to digital creations and IP, assets within Public Network come with full ownership and transparency, enabling seamless exchange, provenance tracking, and attribution.

Information
A decentralized, open source knowledge system that stores data, whether scientific discoveries, articles, news, or digital provenance. Every piece of information is authenticated by its creator or provider through a cryptographic signature, ensuring transparency, accountability, and traceability. This guarantees that data within Public Network remains trustworthy and can be trusted, as every contribution is backed by an authenticated source.

Communities
Decentralized groups and networks where people connect, collaborate, and contribute. These communities are self-organized and govern themselves, enabling participants to engage with shared goals, values, and interests. Privacy and autonomy are respected, while governance is transparent and built on consensus.

Environments
Personalized, decentralized, open source spaces where individuals and communities can interact. These environments offer a platform for expression, sharing, and collaboration, with full control over privacy and visibility, much like digital social profiles but free from centralized control.

Transaction
A secure, decentralized, open source system for exchanging value, goods, and resources, where every transaction is recorded transparently on the blockchain. Whether it’s an exchange of currency or digital assets, the trustless nature of these transactions ensures accountability and security.

Authentication
A decentralized, open source system where users attach their cryptographic signature to approve actions, transactions, or information. This ensures that everything from submitting data to signing agreements is securely tied to the individual, group or institution, allowing for full accountability. With authentication, Public Network reinforces trust by ensuring that every interaction or piece of content is linked to its rightful creator or approver.

Dapps
Decentralized, open source applications that are seamlessly integrated into Public Network’s infrastructure. These apps leverage the network’s security and scalability, enhancing the user experience while providing innovative tools for interaction and collaboration.

Education
A decentralized, open source knowledge-sharing system aimed at empowering users with the skills and resources to navigate and contribute to Public Network. Whether it’s learning how to transfer assets or understanding best practices for interacting within decentralized spaces, this system helps users adapt to a rapidly evolving landscape.

Sustainability
A self-sustaining, open source infrastructure designed to grow and evolve without central oversight. Built as a hyperstructure, Public Network is designed to scale with ongoing contributions, ensuring that it remains resilient and adaptable to future innovations and challenges.

Schemas

The Components of Public Network are realized as five open source schemas on Ethereum. These schemas are public, permissionless, and permanent. They are registered on Optimism and can be written to by any individual, group, or institution with an Ethereum wallet.

IDENTITY_CREATED
A self-owned identity is created by cryptographic signature.

ASSET_CREATED
A real-world, virtual, or intellectual resource is registered to an identity.

INFORMATION_RECORDED
Data, whether scientific discoveries, articles, news, or provenance, is authenticated by its creator’s cryptographic signature and recorded permanently.

TRANSACTION_RECORDED
An exchange of value, goods, or resources is recorded transparently.

AUTHENTICATION_SIGNED
A cryptographic signature approves an action, transaction, or piece of information.

These five schemas together cover the full surface of what Public Network records. They compose. An asset can carry unlimited information. A community is a group of identities. An environment is an asset occupied by identities. Nothing else is needed at the protocol layer.

No Patron can modify, remove, or gate these schemas. No Patron can stop another Patron from writing to them. The schemas were registered once and the deploying key was discarded. They belong to Humanity.

The interface for everything.

The Public Network Interface is open source. Anyone can read, fork, extend, or run their own instance. The canonical Interface is maintained by Patrons.

All applications share the same underlying identities, assets, information, transactions, and authentications. A Patron of one application is the same Patron in every other.

Applications are not approved by Public Network. They do not require permission, registration, or endorsement. They are simply built.

A secure, decentralized, open source system for exchanging value, goods, and resources, where every transaction is recorded transparently on the blockchain. Whether it’s an exchange of currency or digital assets, the trustless nature of these transactions ensures accountability and security.

Authentication
A decentralized, open source system where users attach their cryptographic signature to approve actions, transactions, or information. This ensures that everything from submitting data to signing agreements is securely tied to the individual, group or institution, allowing for full accountability. With authentication, Public Network reinforces trust by ensuring that every interaction or piece of content is linked to its rightful creator or approver.

Dapps
Decentralized, open source applications that are seamlessly integrated into Public Network’s infrastructure. These apps leverage the network’s security and scalability, enhancing the user experience while providing innovative tools for interaction and collaboration.

Education
A decentralized, open source knowledge-sharing system aimed at empowering users with the skills and resources to navigate and contribute to Public Network. Whether it’s learning how to transfer assets or understanding best practices for interacting within decentralized spaces, this system helps users adapt to a rapidly evolving landscape.

Sustainability
A self-sustaining, open source infrastructure designed to grow and evolve without central oversight. Built as a hyperstructure, Public Network is designed to scale with ongoing contributions, ensuring that it remains resilient and adaptable to future innovations and challenges.

Schemas

The Components of Public Network are realized as five open source schemas on Ethereum. These schemas are public, permissionless, and permanent. They are registered on Optimism and can be written to by any individual, group, or institution with an Ethereum wallet.

IDENTITY_CREATED
A self-owned identity is created by cryptographic signature.

ASSET_CREATED
A real-world, virtual, or intellectual resource is registered to an identity.

INFORMATION_RECORDED
Data, whether scientific discoveries, articles, news, or provenance, is authenticated by its creator’s cryptographic signature and recorded permanently.

TRANSACTION_RECORDED
An exchange of value, goods, or resources is recorded transparently.

AUTHENTICATION_SIGNED
A cryptographic signature approves an action, transaction, or piece of information.

These five schemas together cover the full surface of what Public Network records. They compose. An asset can carry unlimited information. A community is a group of identities. An environment is an asset occupied by identities. Nothing else is needed at the protocol layer.

No Patron can modify, remove, or gate these schemas. No Patron can stop another Patron from writing to them. The schemas were registered once and the deploying key was discarded. They belong to Humanity.

Applications

Any Patron can build an application on top of Public Network. Applications read and write the five schemas directly.

A video application. A social application. A photo application. A market application. A knowledge application. A messaging application. A research application. An identity application.

All applications share the same underlying identities, assets, information, transactions, and authentications. A Patron of one application is the same Patron in every other.

Applications are not approved by Public Network. They do not require permission, registration, or endorsement. They are simply built.

The documentation maintains a directory of known applications as a service to Patrons. Inclusion is informational, not gatekept.

The Perfection of the Imperfect Circle

Feeling insecure and alienated from others, the Imperfect Circle tried to join the ranks of the perfect circles, but over time its true self started to diminish along with the traces of its imperfect shape.

After the long quest for an ideally manufactured line, the Imperfect Circle realized that perfection was an abstraction and utopia, an elusive ideal with many interpretations that was impossible to achieve. Eventually, the purest form of perfection was found in its stead: authenticity with all its flaws and mistakes. The Imperfect Circle came back to its innermost being, its original form, unique, real, and free, accepting the innate beauty of itself and others.

But its imperfect shape can only be seen and appreciated by those who are able to look deeper and recognize its true form.

Timeline

A high-level overview of important dates and milestones for Public Network.

Phase 1: Schemas
The five schemas are registered on Optimism. The deploying key is discarded. Public Network becomes ownerless.

Phase 2: Interface
The canonical open source Interface launches at publicnetwork.xyz. The source code is published. Patrons begin contributing.

Phase 3: Applications
Patrons build independent applications on top of Public Network. The ecosystem grows.

Phase 4: Humanity
Public Network becomes the Interstate of Humanity.
